Bio

  • In a full-time capacity, I am a senior quantitative software engineer at Two Sigma.
  • In a part-time capacity, I have taught at Columbia University since Spring 2022 term and I have been a lecturer at MIT since June 2022.
  • In a volunteering capacity, I am a ICPC intern lead, Columbia head coach, a judge for Greater New York Regional, for Mid-Central Regional, and for North America Qualifier, and a practice organizer for Greater New York Regional.
  • I finished my undergraduate education at Stanford University with a double major in mathematics and computer science. Then I finished my co-terminal masters degree in computer science at Stanford University. After that, I pursued a doctorate program in operations research at MIT for about three years prior to joining Google where I stayed for about six years.
