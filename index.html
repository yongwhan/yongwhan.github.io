Bio

  • In a part-time capacity, I have taught at Columbia University since Spring 2022 term and I have been a lecturer at MIT since June 2022.
  • In a volunteering capacity, I am a ICPC coach for Columbia University, a judge for Greater New York and Mid-Central Regionals, a problemsetter for North America Qualifier, and a practice organizer for Greater New York Regional. Also, I am a volunteer for USACO.
  • Previously, I gave lectures at Columbia University, Cornell-Tech, Harvard University, Carnegie Mellon University - Silicon Valley, the University of California System, and the California State University System.
  • I finished my undergraduate education at Stanford University with a double major in mathematics and computer science. Then I finished my co-terminal masters degree in computer science at Stanford University. After that, I pursued a doctorate program in operations research at MIT for about three years prior to joining Google.
